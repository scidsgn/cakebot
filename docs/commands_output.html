$8ball

Provides a specific and on point yes/no answer to a question.

Syntax

$8ball

Parameters

  • question - The question you want to give to the ball.

Examples

Asking a simple question

$8ball Is the sky blue?

Complex queries

$8ball Is the string theory an accurate description of the nature of the Universe?

$roll

Rolls an n-sided die.

Syntax

$roll sides

Parameters

  • sides - The number of the die sides.

Examples

Roll a D6, sis.

$roll 6

Roll a D128, hunty.

$roll 100

$guess

Puts your guess in or modifies your previous guess in a LatePat guessing round.

Syntax

$guess guess

Parameters

  • guess - Your guess, in minutes.

Examples

Guess 5 minutes.

$guess 5

Guess -432.52 minutes.

$guess -432.52

$guesses

Displays a list of all guesses in the current LatePat round, together with some stats.

Syntax

$guesses
